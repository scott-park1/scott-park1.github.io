ABOUT ME

Introduction

Hi everyone, my name is Scott Park. I was born in Korea and moved to New Zealand when I was 8 years old. I am currently based in Auckland. I studied at the University of Auckland, majoring in Economics and Finance and have work experience as a finance team member in different companies.

The reason why I am attending Dev Academy is because I want to develop myself through learning new things that will support me make self improvements and will result as a guide for a successful career. After doing some coding activities online, I now have a growing passion and desire to expand my understanding of coding. After hearing experiences from friends and graduates of Dev Academy, and my friends who are already working as a developer after university, it really clicked into me that I also want to become a developer, and is another contributing factor to why I joined the Academy.

In my spare time, I love to play football with my friends, play games online, and watch movies.

Nice to meet you all!
