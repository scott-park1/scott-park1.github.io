My Learning Plan

What is my long-term goal or career pathway?

I want to develop, learn new things so that I could find some improvements for myself and have a successful career. In order to do that, I would need to study hard, learn as much as I could, and become a good developer with good coding skills in hand.

After getting a job, I want to get competent in the role as soon as possible. Once I am trained up in the role to a good standard, I would like to consolidate my skills and become experienced within the team. It is very important to me to be accepted and respected by my work colleagues and peers. So, I guess the short answer is that I want to become good at my job, be accepted by my team and line managers and enjoy my work as much as possible.

My biggest dream or long-term goal is to be able to take care of my family financially and physically. My parents, when I grew up, they took care of me and supported me in lots of different ways to give me a good life. I want to pay them back, I want to take care of them and just help whenever they need.

A description of my strengths and limitations when it comes to learning.

I have many positive qualities and attributes, but I believe my main key strengths are:

-I have a hard-working nature;

-I have an excellent communication skill;

-I am always looking for the positives in every situation;

-I am a great team player.

One other quality is that I am always looking for ways to improve the way I perform so I can work to the highest standards possible in my role.

I believe all these qualities will help me get through well when it comes to learning. If I was to choose one quality, I would choose to look for positives in every situation and that will be the key to help me to learn as much as I could. I assume there will be some stress as I learn, and I think it is good to have a positive mindset so that I can try to look to find ways to resolve the issue as fast as possible.

I do struggle sometimes, and that is I sometimes get slightly irritated when I use a lot of my time to understand when I do not get the concept. Using a lot of time to understand something can be a good habit but it also means that I will get pressured and will not have a lot of time to finish my other works. Also, because I am a bit introverted and quiet most of the times, I like to be alone and do things by myself. This can cause a lot of problems, like for example, I might try solve the problem by myself even if it takes a lot of time, and might not be brave enough to ask questions to others. I think it will be good to raise a hand to someone for help but when I think about my personality, I am not the person who really does that and might cause some stress when it comes to learning.
